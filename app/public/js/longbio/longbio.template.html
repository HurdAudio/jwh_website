Short Stories

   In addition to the memoir Surprised by Story, Jerrie Hurd has published three novels with Pocket Books, several nonfiction books with a regional publisher as well as short stories and essays. She began her writing career working for her hometown newspaper while still in high school.

   She has taught writing at community colleges in Washington State and New Jersey and lead seminars at writing conferences.

   She is also a fine art photographer whose work has shown in galleries across the U.S.

   Hurd graduated with honors from University of Colorado and got an MFA in creative writing from the University of Oregon.

   She has served on the board of Women of the West Museum and the Autry Museum. She is co-founder and past-president of Women Writing the West and a former board member of Western Writers of America.
