29 Nymphs

 

 

Things to know about a nymph!

No one captures a nymph, she captures you.

A nymph doesn't live forever, just several thousand years.

Nymphs are well disposed towards mortals, but never count on it.

Those who live deep have no patience for shallow water.

There's no such thing as a dry muse.
