56 Ways To Be Naked

 

How often are you naked?

We're born naked, we shower naked, being alone is naked . . .
To get anywhere, you have to let it all hang out . . .
Artists are naked on the canvas . . .
Writers are naked on the page . . .
Musicians make love to every note . . .
Actors have no clothes--only costumes . . .

This 60 page book combines 500 words and 56 photographs to illustrate some of the ways that all of us are exposed most of the time.

It's meant to be a humorous riff that ends on a thoughtful note:

If beauty is skin deep, what's naked?
