Memoir
 

We expect our lives to turn out

like the stories we've heard.

 

  
 

 

Nothing prepares the writer for doing a memoir. So what if you've written novels, nonfiction, short stories, etc. Rattling the skeletons in your own closet requires different nerves.

Just Above Bone refers to where I grew up, in the hills above Bone, Idaho, and where we all live in the larger sense.

The other nonfiction refers to some really old titles, about how women are portrayed in scripture, mentioned for those still looking for them.
